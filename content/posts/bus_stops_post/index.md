Boston 25 News featured Nail Bashan, PhD researcher from Northeastern University, to discuss how stop amenities (like shelter) can impact ridership. The study found that stops with amenities had significantly higher ridership than those without. 

The message to city and transit authorities is loud and clear: Bus journeys begin at the stop!
